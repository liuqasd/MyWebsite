== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 3.0 or greater
* PHP version 5.2.0 or greater

1. Install Heyoya Voice Reviews & Comments either via the [WordPress.org plugin directory](https://wordpress.org/plugins/heyoya-voice-comments-reviews/), or by uploading the files to your server
2. After activating Heyoya Voice Reviews & Comments, go over the Comments link in the left hand side bar of your Wordpress admin and click on the Heyoya entry.
3. You will now need to register an account with Heyoya or log in with your existing account.
4. Customize the different settings on the Heyoya Settings page.
5. That's it.  You're ready to go!

= I'm having difficulties logging in  = 

We use cookies to ensure a secured log-in for you.
If you're experiencing issues while trying to log in, please make sure your browser allows third-party cookies.

Allowing cookies on Chrome:
1. Open the Chrome browser.
2. Go to preferences and select "Settings".
3. Under "Privacy and security", click on "Site Settings" and then "Cookies and other site data".
4. Check "Allow all cookies".

Allowing cookies on Safari:
1. Open the Safari browser.
2. Go to preferences and select "Privacy".
3. Uncheck the following fields:
   * Prevent cross-site tracking.
   * Block all cookies.

Allowing cookies on Firefox:
1. Open the Firefox browser.
2. Go to the "Privacy & Security" section (about:preferences#privacy)
3. Select Custom.
4. Unmark the "Cookies" checkbox.

Your browser is not listed? Contact us at support at heyoya.com, and we'll be happy to offer further assistance.

= Does this plugin fully replace the current comment system? =

Yes! **Voice Comments & Reviews – Heyoya** changes the default comment system to a new interactive system.

= Would the plugin help me to increase my user retention? =

**Voice Comments & Reviews – Heyoya** allows commentators to record short voice posts. Voice comments are easier than typed text for most web users, and there is an element of fun to voice recording that also promotes user engagement. The ease of posting, and the higher engagement rates, will lead naturally to improved user retention, as site visitors who leave comments – in voice or any other format – will naturally be inclined to return and revisit their comments to see responses and follow up. In this way, **Voice Comments & Reviews – Heyoya** voice comments will lead to a natural conversation on the website, keeping your visitors on the site longer, and improving your retention and conversion rates.

= Can I import my existing WordPress comments into Heyoya? =

Yes, you can import your existing WordPress comments into **Voice Comments & Reviews – Heyoya** during installation.

Go to: **Comments** > **Heyoya** > Advanced > Import Comments

= How Heyoya associate comments with my website pages = 

The **Voice Comments & Reviews – Heyoya** plugin is a URL based solution; this means that a comment is associated with the page URL it was posted at.
